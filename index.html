This web site provides comprehensive support for doing every activity carried out by Instrumentation professionals in GNFC.

It has been designed and evolved completely in-house, after continuous efforts of more than five years and presently it has taken shape of a most professionally designed software platform which encompasses all activities of Instrumentation professionals. As a matter of fact, any maintenance department can use it with minor modifications in only some of its modules.

Mission statement:

100% Computerization of all our work practices on a single platform, which is built on a standard DBMS software, is easy to navigate, which meets our customized work requirements, which is platform free as far as the applications are concerned, and, which is 100% secured and authentic as far as data security and integrity are concerned. The system should have standard professional Hardware platform in terms of Server machine, which meets storage reliability, and communication speed requirements.

It has three main sections, namely, Integrated Instrument Management System (IIMS), Utility Section and HELP section.

IIMS section

IIMS section provides platform for Online Operation Job register, E log Book, Engineering documentation, maintenance records and History, Modules for Electronic Lab and Centralized Control Valve Maintenance Service module, Procurement system module, Preventive and other scheduled maintenance modules, archiving of all kinds of communications and correspondences, etc. Powerful search and report generation facilities and reminder systems are built in.

Utility section

Utility section provides supporting information of various kinds, such as brief information of the personnel of Instrument Department, engineering standards and specifications related to Instrumentation work practices, various communication formats, technical literature, information on ISO14000 and SAP, brief information of various plants and product of GNFC etc. It has database on contacts in various other industries. It has a posting area for news and views pertaining to happenings in Instrumentation at GNFC.

HELP section

HELP section provides very elaborate document describing all the features and procedures to put every module to maximum use.
