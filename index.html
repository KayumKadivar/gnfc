Integrated Instrument Management System (IIMS)

IIMS is a comprehensive in-house developed digital platform designed to support and streamline all activities of Instrumentation professionals at GNFC. Built through continuous improvement and innovation, it integrates departmental operations into one secure, efficient, and user-friendly system.

Our Mission

To achieve 100% computerization of Instrumentation work practices on a single, secure, DBMS- based platform that is easy to navigate, customized to our requirements, platform-independent, and fully reliable in terms of data integrity and security.

Main Sections

IIMS Section

The operational core of the department, covering Online Job Register, E-Log Book, engineering documentation, maintenance records and history, electronic lab, control valve services, procurement, preventive maintenance, communication archiving, advanced search, reporting, and automated reminders.

Utility Section

A centralized reference hub providing personnel information, engineering standards, technical literature, ISO/CPCB/SAP references, plant and product details, communication formats, and industry contact database.

Help Section

Comprehensive user guidance to ensure maximum and effective utilization of every module.
